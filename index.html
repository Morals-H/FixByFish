Welcome

Hi, my name is Shawn, I'm an IPC Certified technician based out of Ohio. This is my personal website for sharing projects and interests.


Why make a website?

I like making stuff and sharing my projects, but I don't like the current state of social media. This site is my nerdy little corner of the internet where I can share until my heart's content while avoiding the toxic communities of mainstream social media or the addictive cycle of posting for engagement. If people find my site interesting, it's perfect, but this is more for me than anyone else.
