Why

I hate almost the entirety of social media, and truthfully, what the internet has shifted towards as a whole. Essentially, all major platforms are like slot machines, flashing lights, and ringing bells to keep you in line. If you so much as look away for a minute, these platforms adjust their algorithms to fine-tune the content for you as a person. When it comes to the content they push, some of it is to make you mad, some is to make you sad, and some is to make you laugh. The reason they do this is that they want a rise out of you. An emotional person is an engaged person. These companies have no qualms about damaging your attention span and breaching your privacy as an individual. Therefore, I have no qualms with doing my part to create distance for myself and advocating for others to do the same. FUCK Amazon, Facebook, Google, Microsoft, Flock, and every other company that seeks to deanonomise private citizens. There are so many excellent alternatives that value your privacy as an individual, and I want to talk about them all.
