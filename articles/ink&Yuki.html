Ink & Yuki

    11/16/2024

    Project

    Ink & Yuki is a cooperative collectathon game published on June 15, 2022, made for a two-week Game Jam called Meow Jam.


    Technical

    Ink & Yuki was made in Unity; I wrote the game almost entirely in C# and utilized Photon Pun 2 hosting multiplayer servers.

    I also created the art entirely from scratch because I wanted something entirely unique, and I think I achieved my goal. The game is monochrome in a 3D space with cute cartoony visuals. I had many challenges in making the game visually readable; some of my solutions can be seen with the outlines, the distance particles, and the landing shadow.


    What Happened

    At one point, we were in talks with a publisher, but they needed a much quicker turnout than my team could handle, so we had to decline. Since then, nothing has happened with the game.


    Final Thoughts

    Ink & Yuki is a project I look back on very fondly. I've been interested in continuing it since, but I failed at producing anything as special as I did in the first two weeks. It might be something I revisit in the future, but as it stands, it's among my favorite projects and has a special place in my heart.

    The game is playable here, a short demo with a lot of jankyness.
