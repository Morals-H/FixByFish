Artificial technology (AI) is a great tool, nothing more, nothing less. I've used it in a great few projects; hell, it assisted in creating this website. At the time of creation, I had experience in Unity C# and JavaScript, but by no means was I then, nor am I now, a software developer, nor do I have the ambition to be. I used ChatGPT to do the key structure, nd I worked from there. If I had it to do over again, I would have done more by hand, but that's because I learned from working with AI to create the project to begin with.

With that said, AI should never take the place of your voice as an individual. I believe AI use is ethical only dependent on your intent as a creator. While I enjoy making and working on software, it is not my art, but sharing my cause and projects is. While some of my articles are currently AI-written, I'm going back and rewriting these to truly make them my own.
